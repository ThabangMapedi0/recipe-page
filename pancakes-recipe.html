Classic Pancakes

A simple and delicious pancake recipe perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until combined.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and grease lightly with butter or oil.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.
